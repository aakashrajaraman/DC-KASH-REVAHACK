A NEW AGE WASTE DETECTION & MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Aiming to combat the ever present and unfortunately growing problem of rampant and unmonitored waste disposal, SpyTrash aims to put the power of knowledge in your hands. Using your device's in built camera and our classification and networking system, we give you the profile of the waste you detect and the best course of action to take.

Our solution uses Computer Vision paired with Artificial Intelligence to detect and classify various types of waste and provide optimal solution for handling and disposal. The WebApp includes services such as GeoLocation, Encyclopaedia integration along with various other safety parameters such as flammability and toxicity indicators.
